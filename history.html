Branching Out

In 2014, Shazam collaborated with Digimarc, a company that developed image recognition techniques to aid on object scanning. Using Shazam's visual recognizer can link you immediately to related websites.

Future

Shazam CEO, Rich Riley: "The current Shazam app is fantastic for kind of developed world conditions, but not necessarily ideal for emerging markets, and so there's another billion to go get there. Then, the other addition is a strategy where we're going to be embedding Shazam into other applications, and so you'll see over the coming months several examples where while you're inside another application, it could be a messaging application, it could be a social application, it could be a video application, that you could just Shazam from within that experience, and so making it just easier to use Shazam without having to leave the experience that you're in."
